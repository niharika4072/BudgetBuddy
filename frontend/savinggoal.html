💡 Smart Saving Tips

⚡

Pay yourself first. Transfer your monthly contribution on the 1st before spending anything else.

📈

Use a Recurring Deposit. Park goal money in an RD — it earns 5–7% interest and is harder to spend impulsively.

🎯

Break big goals small. A ₹60,000 goal feels huge. ₹5,000/month for 12 months feels achievable.

🏆

Celebrate milestones. Hit 25%, 50%, 75%? Acknowledge it — motivation compounds too.
