💡 Emergency Fund — Smart Tips
🏦

Keep it liquid

Your emergency fund must be accessible within 24 hours. A savings account or liquid mutual fund is ideal — not locked FDs.

🔒

Keep it separate

Never mix emergency money with your regular spending account. Out of sight = out of temptation.

🎯

3 months first

Start with 1 month as a quick goal, then push to 3 months. 6 months is the gold standard for financial security.

🔄

Replenish after use

If you use your emergency fund, treat rebuilding it as your top financial priority — before any other goals.

📈

Earn interest too

A Sweep-in FD or Liquid Mutual Fund earns 5–7% on your emergency fund while keeping it fully accessible.

⚠️

What counts as an emergency?

Job loss, medical bill, urgent travel, appliance breakdown. A sale or party does NOT count as an emergency.

⚠️ Withdraw from Emergency Fund

Only use this for a genuine emergency. Withdrawals will reduce your safety net and should be replenished as soon as possible.
