Why It Matters

Why should you track money?

🎓
As a Student

Build habits that last a lifetime

  • Understand where your pocket money or stipend actually goes each month.
  • Avoid running out of money before the month ends.
  • Learn to distinguish needs from wants early on.
  • Start saving for goals like gadgets, trips, or future studies.
  • Develop financial discipline that sets you apart after college.
💼
As a Young Adult

Turn income into lasting wealth

  • Track multiple income sources and manage variable expenses.
  • Plan for big milestones — a car, a home, or starting a business.
  • Build an emergency fund that protects you from financial shocks.
  • Understand your spending patterns and cut what doesn't serve you.
  • Make informed investment decisions with clear savings data.
